Bhedhiya(Film)

Bhedhiya is indian hindi-language comedy horror film directed by Amar Kaushik produced by Dinesh Vijan. It 
stars Varun Dhavan and Kriti Sanon in lead roles. It is the third installment of Vijans Horror-Comedy Universe.
November 2022 and positive riviews from audience and critics alike.


 Production

The producer of film, Dinesh Vijan, confirmed that Bhediya is part of his horror comedy universe consisting 
of films like Stree, Roohi and Munjha. Munjha will be the prequel to 2018 film Stree.  The shooting for 
Bhedhiya is done in the region of Arunachal Pradesh.
