Avtar:The Way Of Water

Avatar: The Way of Water is a 2022 American epic science fiction film and the sequel to the 2009 film Avatar.
both directed by James Cameron.The film is distributed by 20th Century Studios, as the second installment in
the Avtar film series.


 Production

In 2006, James Cameron stated that if Avatar (2009) was successful, he hoped to make two sequels to the film.
In 2010, he said the film's widespread success confirmed that he would do so. The sequels were originally
scheduled for release in December 2014 and 2015.

 Soundtrack

 Avatar composer James Horner was originally reported to score music for the franchise, before his death
 in a plane crash in June 2015.
